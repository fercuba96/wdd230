Site Plan

Site Name

  • Name: Huachipa Chamber of Commerce, this is the name of the locale where is located the chamber and main office.

Site Purpose

  • The Huachipa Chamber of Commerce supports bakeries in the city, fostering quality and development in bread and bakery product production. The chamber and its website serve as platforms for notifying the community about the achievements and advancements made over the years.

Scenarios

  • 1. What are the main products that this chamber supports? 2.What are the main goals for the first year of operation? 3.What is population growth in Huachipa? 4.Who are the main stakeholders of this chamber?
